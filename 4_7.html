The World's Quietest Room

Silence is golden, as they say. And while it may not be worth quite as much as jewels and gold to most people, it certainly was the primary goal for those who built the quietest room in the world. Located at Microsoft's headquarters in Redmond, Washington State, the lab room measures a background noise of -20.35 dBA, which is 20 decibels below the threshold of human hearing and breaking previous records for spaces that were deemed the planet's quietest places, according to CNN.
